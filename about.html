BIG SOUND, SMALL BAND.

Formed in August 2022 and based out of Boise, Itsy Bitsy consists of:

Tovi Chance - Lead Vocals
Lilly Furlow - Rhythm Guitar
Maz Della Badia - Lead Guitar
Jack Tiderman - Bass
Brian Dyer - Drums

They released their five-track debut self-titled EP on December 21st, 2024. Having just played at Treefort Music Fest 2025, the band is hard at work playing shows and writing new material.
